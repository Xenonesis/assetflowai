# Section 16 — Enterprise Risk Management, Governance & Business Continuity

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines the Enterprise Risk Management (ERM), Governance, and Business Continuity framework for AssetFlow AI.

As the platform scales across customers, regions, AI providers, and enterprise deployments, proactive risk management becomes a strategic capability rather than a reactive process.

This chapter establishes governance structures, risk ownership, compliance oversight, operational resilience, and business continuity to ensure sustainable growth and enterprise trust.

---

# Chapter Objectives

The Enterprise Governance Platform must:

✓ Identify enterprise risks

✓ Assess business impact

✓ Define mitigation strategies

✓ Establish governance processes

✓ Ensure regulatory compliance

✓ Support business continuity

✓ Improve organizational resilience

✓ Enable executive oversight
