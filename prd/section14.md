# Section 14 — Product Analytics, Intelligence & Business Insights

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines the Product Analytics, Business Intelligence, and Decision Intelligence framework for AssetFlow AI.

The platform continuously measures user behavior, feature adoption, operational efficiency, AI effectiveness, customer success, and business outcomes through a unified analytics architecture.

Rather than collecting data for reporting alone, AssetFlow AI transforms operational data into actionable business intelligence that supports executives, managers, administrators, and AI agents.

---

# Chapter Objectives

The Product Intelligence Platform must:

✓ Measure product adoption

✓ Understand customer behavior

✓ Improve user experience

✓ Optimize AI performance

✓ Support executive decision-making

✓ Enable data-driven product development

✓ Predict business trends

✓ Deliver enterprise dashboards
