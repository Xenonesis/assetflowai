# Section 20 — Enterprise Architecture Decisions, Technical Governance & Future Evolution

---

# Purpose

This chapter establishes the architectural governance framework for AssetFlow AI.

As the platform evolves over multiple years, engineering teams, architects, AI engineers, DevOps engineers, and enterprise customers require a structured record of architectural decisions, technology standards, trade-offs, modernization strategy, and long-term technical direction.

Rather than documenting implementation details, this chapter captures the reasoning behind major technical decisions and provides governance for future architectural evolution.

---

# Chapter Objectives

The Architecture Governance Platform must:

✓ Document architecture decisions

✓ Standardize technology selection

✓ Reduce technical debt

✓ Maintain architectural consistency

✓ Enable future modernization

✓ Support engineering onboarding

✓ Improve long-term maintainability

✓ Preserve engineering knowledge
