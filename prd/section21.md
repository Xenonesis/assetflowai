# Section 21 — Enterprise Product Operations, Commercial Strategy & Business Growth

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines how AssetFlow AI operates as a commercial enterprise product after successful engineering implementation.

While previous chapters define how the platform is designed, built, deployed, secured, and governed, this chapter defines how the business grows, acquires customers, monetizes the platform, manages enterprise accounts, expands globally, and sustains long-term profitability.

This chapter transforms AssetFlow AI from an engineering platform into a scalable software business.

---

✓ Define commercialization strategy
