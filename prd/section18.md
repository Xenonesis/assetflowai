# Section 18 — Enterprise Operations, Customer Success & Platform Lifecycle

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines how AssetFlow AI operates after reaching production.

While previous chapters focus on designing, building, securing, testing, and deploying the platform, this chapter focuses on operating, supporting, evolving, and growing the platform throughout its lifecycle.

This ensures AssetFlow AI remains reliable, customer-focused, scalable, and continuously improving throughout its operational life.

---

# Chapter Objectives

The Operations Platform must:

✓ Deliver world-class customer support

✓ Standardize operational workflows

✓ Enable customer onboarding

✓ Improve adoption

✓ Manage enterprise accounts

✓ Measure customer success

✓ Support long-term platform evolution

✓ Build operational excellence
