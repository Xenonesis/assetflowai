# Section 28 — Corporate Governance, Organizational Design & Operating Model

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines how the organization behind AssetFlow AI is structured, governed, and operated.

As the company scales, effective governance, organizational design, leadership structures, operational policies, and decision-making frameworks become critical to sustaining innovation, execution, and enterprise trust.

This chapter establishes the company's operating model.

---

# Chapter Objectives

The Organizational Platform must:

✓ Define organizational structure

✓ Standardize governance

✓ Improve decision-making

✓ Support scalable leadership

✓ Foster engineering excellence

✓ Enable cross-functional collaboration

✓ Build organizational resilience

✓ Support sustainable growth
