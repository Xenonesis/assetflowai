# Section 13.10 — Quality Metrics & Continuous Improvement

---

# Purpose

This section defines how quality is measured, monitored, governed, and continuously improved throughout the lifecycle of AssetFlow AI.

Quality is treated as a measurable business capability supported by engineering metrics, AI evaluation, operational telemetry, customer feedback, and continuous optimization.

---

# Objectives

The Quality Platform must:

✓ Measure quality continuously

✓ Track engineering health

✓ Improve release quality

✓ Monitor AI performance

✓ Reduce escaped defects

✓ Optimize customer satisfaction

✓ Drive continuous improvement

✓ Support executive reporting
