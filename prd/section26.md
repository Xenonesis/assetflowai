# Section 26 — Enterprise AI Research, Model Strategy & Future Intelligence

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines the long-term Artificial Intelligence research strategy for AssetFlow AI.

Rather than depending solely on third-party AI providers, the platform establishes a structured framework for model evaluation, AI research, multi-model orchestration, autonomous systems, and future AI capabilities.

The objective is to ensure AssetFlow AI remains AI-native as the global AI ecosystem evolves.

---

# Chapter Objectives

The AI Research Platform must:

✓ Evaluate foundation models

✓ Optimize model routing

✓ Research new AI capabilities

✓ Improve autonomous agents

✓ Reduce AI costs

✓ Increase AI accuracy

✓ Future-proof the AI platform
